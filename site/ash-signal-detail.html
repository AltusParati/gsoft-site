Development Notes

Latest work

This area collects the latest design decisions, gameplay progress, and pre-release notes for Ash & Signal.

March 28, 2026

The core gameplay loop was locked

  • The gather, repair, and expand structure was finalized as the main rhythm
  • Each new structure now opens a system, area, or resource by design

March 26, 2026

Region types and stage order were outlined

  • Research grounds, dark marshes, scrap zones, and abandoned islands were chosen as the first world set
  • Each stage is planned to carry its own restoration identity

March 24, 2026

The settlement growth fantasy was framed

  • The journey arc now grows from a campfire into colonies, mechanical cities, and living harbor towns
  • The pacing is built around the player's curiosity about what unlocks next
