Pre-release Privacy Note

Anilar Muzesi

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Anilar Muzesi is still in development. This page outlines the planned privacy approach before release. The content will be updated before the game goes live based on the final services, permissions, and data flow.

Project status

This text is a draft. The final version will be expanded based on the active features in the release build and updated together with the store version.

Planned data approach

The goal is not to collect personal data that is not required for gameplay. If player preferences or progress records are needed, the priority is to keep them on the device whenever possible.

Possible permissions

  • Camera, microphone, or location permissions are not currently planned as a required part of the experience.
  • If sharing, export, or media-related features are added later, the required permissions will be clearly listed.

Third-party services

  • If analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or cloud save services are used, they will be added to this page before release.
  • Google Play or related platform services may apply their own privacy policies separately.

Updates

Visuals, store links, and privacy details will be updated here as the project moves closer to release. The date on this page will be refreshed whenever there is an important change.
